Welcome to Scratch!

Click on any of the projects in the projects section and leave a comment in the comments section to meet a member of the Welcoming Committee!

To talk to people and advertise your projects, please go to the correct section of the discussion forums:
http://scratch.mit.edu/discuss/8

To find out how to use the discussion forums, look at this project: https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/168332076/
=====================
WANT TO JOIN?

To join, go to the “Project Submissions - Welcoming Committee” studio (link below) and follow the instructions in the description.

Project Submissions - Welcoming Committee studio: https://scratch.mit.edu/studios/4276585/
=====================
WANT TO CURATE?
We are not looking for curators at the moment. If you still want to help out, you can become a member of the welcoming committee, answer to comments and give feedback on Scratchers' projects.
======================
NEED HELP?

- Tips page: https://scratch.mit.edu/tips

- Questions about Scratch: https://scratch.mit.edu/discuss/4/

- Scratch Wiki: https://en.scratch-wiki.info/

- Help with Scripts: https://scratch.mit.edu/discuss/7/
